What is Kintsugi?

Repairing with Gold

Kintsugi (金継ぎ) is the centuries-old Japanese art of repairing broken ceramics with lacquer mixed with gold, silver, or platinum powder. Rather than concealing the damage, it illuminates the cracks — transforming scars into seams of gold.

The crack is not the wound — it is the map.
